What I Do

Legend and folklore heavily inspire my work– from anicient mythology to urban legends, there is something about the unexplained and uncanny is deeply fascinating to the human mind.

Scientific and fantastical illustration seem to effortlessly turn into its own "creature" in my head. By mixing digital and traditional mediums, a unique variety of illustrative styles can be created.Then its all about combining that with non-western art and pulling elements from comic books and animation.

Fantastical beasts, occult creatures, anthopomorphic characters all combine into a sequential narrative to build its own reality.
